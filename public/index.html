How It Works

Step 1: Ghost-me-not
Log a date. We recommend talking about it with your date first. (Tip: We make a good conversation topic on a first date.)

Step 2: Be open
Answer some questions about how your date went. Your answers will only be revealed to your date if they answer the same ones.

Step 3: Good luck
Once your both answer questions, take a look and see where you both stand.
